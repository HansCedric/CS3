One Piece

One Piece follows a group of pirates led by a rubber boy with a straw hat as they adventure the world. The crew's individual goals vary, but all of them wholeheartedly support the aspiration of their captain - become the pirate king by finding the treasure left behind by Gold Roger the last pirate king. During their adventures they meet, befriend, and fight many people, some include: Fish People, Candy People, Talking Animals, Talking Skeletons, Kings, Corrupt Governments and many many more.
